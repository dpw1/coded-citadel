## The premise

I'm Diego — a senior software engineer with over a decade of experience. I've wanted to run a "zero to X dollars" project for years, but I never committed to a format that felt honest.

This site and my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@CodedCitadel) are that commitment.

## What I'm shipping

My first products are Chrome extensions aimed at real workflow pain:

- **YouTube Filter Pro** — filter search results by duration, views, date, and more
- **Claude Deep Search** — full-text search across Claude conversations, fully local
- **YouTube Comments Exporter** — export comments for analysis or archival
- **Instagram DM Exporter** — export DM threads as structured files

Each one solves a problem I actually had. If it helps you too, that's the business model for now: free extensions, figure out monetization later.

## What "building in public" means here

I'm not selling a course about how I made money in 30 days. You'll see:

- Real install counts and active user numbers on this site
- Weekly progress updates on YouTube
- Blog posts when I learn something worth sharing

Some weeks will be quiet. Some launches will flop. That's the deal.

## Follow along

Subscribe on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@CodedCitadel) or bookmark this blog. The journey is the product until the products pay the bills.
